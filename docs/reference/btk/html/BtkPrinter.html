btk_printer_get_hard_margins ()

gboolean
btk_printer_get_hard_margins (BtkPrinter *printer,
                              gdouble *top,
                              gdouble *bottom,
                              gdouble *left,
                              gdouble *right);

Retrieve the hard margins of printer , i.e. the margins that define the area at the borders of the paper that the printer cannot print to.

Note: This will not succeed unless the printer's details are available, see btk_printer_has_details() and btk_printer_request_details().

Parameters

[out]

[out]

right

a location to store the right margin in.

[out]

Returns

TRUE iff the hard margins were retrieved
